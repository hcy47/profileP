Sunset Market

Welcome to 'Sunset Market' main page. This is where you get all the updates about our beloved sunset market every month.

The Oceanside Sunset Market is a weekly street fair held in downtown Oceanside, California, every Thursday evening. It's a popular event that combines elements of a farmers market, street fair, and family-friendly entertainment venue, attracting both locals and visitors. The market features a wide array of international food vendors, live music, and unique shopping opportunities.
